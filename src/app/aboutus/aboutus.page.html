Second-Generation Family Farm dotted line In 1976 our, Grandfather Epineri Toduadua, graduate student, of FNU Agriculture College borrowed money from his parents to buy 20 acres of property in Tailevu. Today, we continue to grow our family on the farm, encouraging strong roots for a better food and agriculture system for all. Although Wini's Vege has evolved to best serve the needs of todays busy consumer, our brand integrity has remained intact. A nostalgic experience for multiple generations of Tailevuans, we are now franchising our beloved brand, bringing the Farm Stores experience to communities across Fiji and beyond.
